About

Hi!

I’m Floriane Woodley. An illustrator with a unique style characterised by soft and sketchy lines. This allows me to produce playful and colourful illustrations that reflect my curiosity about the world and my love for travel. I specialise in creating illustrations with extraordinary colour palettes. Through my structured approach and conceptual work, my illustrations are well developed and to the point. With my versatile works, I aim to take the viewer on a journey and convey my passion for the world to them.

In the future, I hope to touch, enchant, and provoke thought in even more people through my illustrations, designs, and graphic recordings.
